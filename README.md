# The Chapel - By Qeutron

## Mission Briefing
In the eastern foothills of Appalachia, a decrepit chapel has become all the gossip for the nearby locals.

Although it has long been known to house a strange cult of recluses, there has been a growing number of unusual sightings near the area.
For reasons unknown to you, the FBI has taken notice of these reports; weirder still, this upcoming operation has been planned in utmost secrecy. 

The coordinator has given you only two adamant indications:
1) Don't get caught
2) Burn down your target.
You are now on site with 3 other agents of the FBI SWAT division, who are here to assist only should the worst come to pass. You are doubtful, but deep down apprehensive. Let's hope Appalachia doesn't live up to its reputation.

## Gameplay Overview
- A small, linear mission. Although not overly difficult, you are expected to have a good hold of EPFC's mechanics. 'Unconventional' solutions are sometimes required.
- Stealth is required
- No forced loadout or perks.
